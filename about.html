About Hackademics

We help you win hacks

We demystify the hackathon circuit, forgea stronger tech community, and give you the tools and team needed to win in competitions and in your career.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24-48 hour build sprint where multidisciplinary teams bypass the typical process and focus only on building a functional prototype or solution. It's the fastest way to learn skills, forge connections, and add a winning project to your portfolio.
